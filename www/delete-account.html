Delete Account & Personal Data

Last updated: August 22, 2025

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to request deletion of your Beopp account and associated personal data. After we verify your request, we'll permanently delete or anonymize your data, except where retention is required by law (e.g., payment and fraud revention records).

Quick options

  1. Email us: Send a message to support@beopp.com with the subject "Delete My Beopp Account." Include the email address tied to your account.
  2. Or submit the form below: We'll email you to confirm before proceeding.

Request form

Note: This simple form sends an email to our support team. If you prefer, email us directly at support@beopp.com.

What will be deleted?

  • Profile information (name, username, profile photo, social sign-in identifiers).
  • Contest entries, comments, and votes associated with your account (where feasible).
  • Access tokens and authentication data.

We may retain certain transactional records (e.g., amounts and timestamps) for tax, accounting, chargeback, or fraud prevention purposes, as permitted by law. These records are not used to identify you after deletion.

How long does it take?

We usually process verified requests within 30 days. Some backups or logs may take longer to cycle out, but we stop using your data immediately after deletion.
