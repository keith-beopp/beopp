Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Beopp collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you use the Service. By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Quick summary

  • We collect account details you provide and limited data from social sign-in (Google/Facebook) if you choose to use it.
  • Payments are processed by Stripe; we don't store full card numbers.
  • We use cookies/analytics to keep you signed in and improve the Service.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

1. Information we collect

  • Account & profile information. Name, email address, username, password (if using Beopp credentials), and any content you submit (entries, photos, comments).
  • Social sign-in data. If you sign in with Google or Facebook via AWS Cognito, we receive information such as your name, email, and profile picture as permitted by the provider and your settings. You can disconnect at any time via those providers.
  • Payment information. When you purchase votes or other items, Stripe processes your payment. We receive transaction metadata (amount, status, last4, card brand) but not full card numbers.
  • Usage & device data. IP address, browser type, device identifiers, pages viewed, and interactions, collected via server logs and cookies.
  • Support communications. Messages you send us (e.g., email, forms) and related metadata.

2. How we use information

  • Provide, secure, and maintain the Service (including authentication, fraud prevention, and abuse detection).
  • Operate contests, process votes, show results, and deliver prizes.
  • Process payments and prevent chargeback or fraud (via Stripe).
  • Communicate with you about your account, entries, or policy updates.
  • Analyze usage to improve features and performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

3. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

We process personal data under: (a) contract (to provide the Service), (b) legitimate interests (e.g., security, improvements), (c) consent (e.g., marketing where applicable), and (d) legal obligations.

4. Sharing of information

We share information with trusted providers that help us operate the Service, subject to appropriate safeguards:

  • Authentication: AWS Cognito (Google and Facebook social login).
  • Payments: Stripe (checkout and webhooks).
  • Infrastructure & storage: Hosting, databases, logging, and security tools.
  • Compliance & legal: When required by law, to protect rights, or as part of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

5. Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences, and understand usage. You can control cookies via your browser settings; disabling certain cookies may limit functionality (e.g., login).

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Payment records may be retained for tax, accounting, and fraud-prevention requirements.

7. Your choices & rights

  • Access, update, or delete certain profile information in your account.
  • Disconnect Google or Facebook sign‑in from their respective settings.
  • Opt out of non‑essential emails via unsubscribe links.
  • Request access, correction, portability, or deletion by contacting us (see “Contact us”). EEA/UK/California residents may have additional rights under GDPR/UK GDPR/CPRA.

8. Children’s privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us to request deletion.

9. International transfers

We may process and store information in the United States and other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

10. Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical measures designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Data deletion instructions

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by visiting https://www.beopp.com/delete-account.html or emailing support@beopp.com with the subject “Delete My Data”. We will verify your request and delete or anonymize data consistent with applicable law (note: we may retain limited records where required for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes).
